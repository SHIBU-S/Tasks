CHOCOLATE:

Chocolate, or cocoa, is a food made from roasted and ground cacao seed kernels that is available as a liquid, solid, or paste, either on its own or as a flavoring agent in other foods.

Types of chocolate:

Chocolate is a food product made from roasted and ground cocoa pods mixed with fat (e.g. cocoa butter) and powdered sugar to produce a solid confectionery. There are several types of chocolate, classified primarily according to the proportion of cocoa and fat content used in a particular formulation.

Some of them are,
  1. Dark Chocolate
  2. Milk Chocolate
  3. White Chocolate
  4. Ruby Chocolate
  5. Raw Chocolate
