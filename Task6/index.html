    T20I MATCH

    Twenty20 (T20) is a shortened game format of cricket.

    At the professional level, it was introduced by the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB) in 2003 for the inter-county competition.

    In a Twenty20 game, the two teams have a single innings each, which is restricted to a maximum of twenty overs.


    ODI MATCH

    A One Day International (ODI) is a form of 50 overs limited overs cricket

    The international one-day game is a late-twentieth-century development.

    One Day International matches are also called Limited Overs Internationals (LOI), although this generic term may also refer to Twenty20 International matches.
